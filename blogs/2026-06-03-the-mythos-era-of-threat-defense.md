As of May 22, 2026, Censys's Mythos Preview had identified approximately 6,202 high- or critical-severity vulnerabilities across more than 1,000 open-source projects, with fewer than one percent receiving patches or public disclosures. AI automation has extended cybercriminal capabilities across reconnaissance, testing, exploit development, and operational execution, making speed the defining variable in the attacker-defender competition. Censys positions its continuous internet scanning as an early-warning system that sees exposures and adversary infrastructure before they are weaponized.
