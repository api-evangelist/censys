May 29 , 13:00 EDT Resolved - Censys has identified and remediated an issue that affected Data Download snapshot generation on May 29, 2026. As a result, we are unable to regenerate the May 29th snapshot, and it has been removed from the list of available snapshots in the current download endpoints. Data Downloads for the Universal Internet Dataset and Certificate Dataset were not affected and remained available throughout.
