Security teams struggle to synthesize sufficient context for rapid, accurate decision-making when toggling between multiple consoles, especially when SOC analysts need external data to triage whether an indicator of compromise warrants deeper investigation. Censys outlines how enriching SIEM and SOAR alerts with internet intelligence — covering asset status, network ownership, certificates, DNS, running services, and historical infrastructure appearances — enables faster and more accurate defensive decisions. The platform addresses the balance between proactive and reactive security measures for both human and AI-driven analysts.
